More About Me

I have been working on and with computers since I was 10 when I built my very first custom rig. From there I self taught HTML and started building websites on AOL and GeoCities. I created multiple DBZ RPG pages and continued my love for computers experimenting and trying new things.

Eventually, I received my Associates in Computer Programming and Systems Design from American Business and Technical University. I learned Java and VB.Net while using Visual Studio. Creating forms is one of my favorite things to do.

Along the way I learned Unity and Blender and decided that I wanted to become a Game Developer. I've also experimented with Arduino where I've programmed automated robots and green houses using Servos, DC Motors and different Sensors. One of my favorite creations was my Beer Bot!
